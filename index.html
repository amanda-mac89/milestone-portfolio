About Me

Hi! I'm Amanda,

I'm a front-end web developer living & creating in Houston, Texas. I have a passion for clean & colorful design. Check out some of my work in my portfolio!

When I'm not writing code, I enjoy spending time with my little family. I love all things health & fitness, travel, fashion, food, and design. You can find me getting my hands dirty in my garden or kitchen. Much to my husband's dismay, I am ALWAYS in the middle of a DIY project. And I love it.

In all of my projects, I aim to write clean code that is easy to read & maintain in order to promote collaboration with other developers. I strive to keep up with best practices in semantic HTML and CSS in order to create websites built for the modern world.
